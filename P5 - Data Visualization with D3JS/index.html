Thermic power produced by a heat pumps

Thermic pumps use electric power to rise or lower the temperature of a thermo-vector (usually water).

The following charts shows the water input (temp_i) and output (temp_o) temperature. The area between temp_i and temo_o is coloured by the heating (red) or cooling (blue) thermic power given from the heat pump.

Thermic power produced by "on/off" thermic pumps

On/Off pumps are unable to modulate the compressor power. To mantain the water temperature in a certain temperature range those pumps have to sitch on and off.
