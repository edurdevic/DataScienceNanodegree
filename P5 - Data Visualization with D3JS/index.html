Thermic power produced by heat pumps

Thermic pumps use electric power to rise or lower the temperature of a thermo-vector (usually water) flowing through the heat pump.

The following charts shows the input (temp_i) and output (temp_o) temperature of the water flowing through the heat pump. The area between temp_i and temop_o is coloured by the heating (red) or cooling (blue) thermic power provided.
